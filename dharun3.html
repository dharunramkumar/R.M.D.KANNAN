Home Page
Virudhunagar Hindu Nadars' Senthikumara Nadar College is a general degree college located in Virudhunagar, Tamil Nadu. It was established in the year 1947. The college is affiliated with Madurai Kamaraj University. The college is ranked 95th among colleges in India by the NIRF in 2024.
